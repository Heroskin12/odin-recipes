Honey Mustard Grilled Chicken

Description

'If ye have faith as a grain of mustard seed', ye shall make and enjoy this simple, tangy, delicious grilled chicken dish!

This recipe can be completed within 35 minutes and can serve up to 4 people.

Ingredients

  • Dijon Mustard
  • Honey
  • Mayonnaise
  • Steak Sauce
  • Chicken Breast Halves

Steps

  1. Preheat the grill for medium heat.

  2. In a shallow bowl, mix the mustard, honey, mayonnaise and steak sauce. Set aside a small amount of the honey mustard sauce for basting, and dip the chicken into the remaining sauce to coat.

  3. Lightly oil the grill grate. Grill chicken over indirect heat for 18 to 20 minutes, turning occassionally, or until juices run clear. Baste occasionally with the reserved sauce during the last 10 minutes. Watch carefully to prevent burning!
